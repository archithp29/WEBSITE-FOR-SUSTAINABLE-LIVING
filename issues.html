Act Now for a Sustainable Future!!

These issues arise from the unsustainable practices and systems
that dominate various aspects of our society.
Here are some key issues related to sustainable living:

1.Climate Change: The increasing concentration of greenhouse gases in the atmosphere, primarily
due to human activities, is leading to global warming and climate change. This poses significant
risks to ecosystems, biodiversity, and human well-being.
2. Deforestation: Rampant deforestation for agriculture, logging, and urbanization contributes to
habitat loss, biodiversity decline, and increased carbon dioxide levels in the atmosphere. Preserving
and restoring forests is crucial for carbon sequestration and maintaining ecosystem health.
3.Pollution: Various forms of pollution, such as air pollution, water pollution, and soil contamination,
pose significant threats to human health, ecosystems, and biodiversity. Reducing pollution levels
is essential for sustainable living.
